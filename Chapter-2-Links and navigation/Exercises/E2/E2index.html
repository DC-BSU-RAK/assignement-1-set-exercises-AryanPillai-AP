Overview

*Spider-Man (PS4)* is an action-adventure video game developed by Insomniac Games and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It follows an experienced Peter Parker as he battles classic and original villains while balancing his dual life as Spider-Man and a civilian.

Key Info

  • Platform: PlayStation 4
  • Developer: Insomniac Games
  • Publisher: Sony Interactive Entertainment
  • Release Date: September 7, 2018
  • Genre: Action-Adventure

Cast & Creative Team

  • Peter Parker / Spider-Man – Yuri Lowenthal
  • Mary Jane Watson – Laura Bailey
  • Otto Octavius – William Salyers
  • Creative Director – Bryan Intihar
  • Writer – Jon Paquette

Characters

  • Spider-Man / Peter Parker
  • Mary Jane Watson
  • Doctor Octopus (Otto Octavius)
  • Miles Morales
  • Kingpin
  • Mister Negative

Reviews

Critics praised the game for its fluid web-swinging mechanics, engaging story, and impressive visuals. It received a 87/100 on Metacritic and was widely regarded as one of the best superhero games ever made.
