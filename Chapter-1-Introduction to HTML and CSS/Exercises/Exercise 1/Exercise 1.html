Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's creamy, comforting, and incredibly quick to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the pasta cooks, fry the pancetta in a pan until crispy. Add garlic and saut√© for a minute.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in the grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving a little of the cooking water.
  5. Quickly toss the hot pasta with the pancetta and garlic, then remove from heat.
  6. Stir in the egg and cheese mixture, adding a splash of pasta water if needed to make it creamy.
  7. Season with salt and plenty of black pepper. Serve immediately.
